Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Salt
  • Water

Instruction

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water
    1. Add salt to taste
    2. Cook for 10 minutes, String occasionally
  3. Add Marinara souce to a pan, Brining to a simmer
  4. Enjoy and eat
